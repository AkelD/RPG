Magic

Before listing the spells themselves, it's worth noting that not all species cast magic the same way, and there isn't a universal school of magic. Every method has adventages and disadventages.
Mana burnout bonuses only affect those who suffered the mana burnout due to using a spell with that method.
Note about classification: Offensive spells are stat damagers. Deffensive spells reduce damage received. Pasive spells are invocations or stat restorers/boosters. Miscelanous spells are their namesake, such as telekinesis or teleportation, even if they can be used ofensively or defensively.
Note about mana burnout stacking: it doesn't stack over itself. For example, the effects of Arcane Magic mana burnout won't be doubled by the *2 multiplier.
